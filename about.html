Some Data Visualisation Examples

1.The picture below is an interactive figure which displyas intercity bus passenger distribution of three major cities in Jiangsu province, China

2.The figure below shows how a bunch of taxi drivers started their business in a day in Chengdu,China

Most taximen chose the most convient routes driving from the rural area(where they live in) to the downtown where they think is more likely to find their first passenger. While some prefered to make a detour, they may think they can find their very first potential customer in other parts of this city in the early morning.

3. The Nanjing Bikesharing System has expanded dramatically since March,2016.

4. The impact of adverse weather on bikesharing usage during peak hours in March,2016.

As shown in the figure above, most of the docking stations not significantly affected by any adverse weather factors (stations denoted by blue dots) are located in the areas where the bike usage is pretty high. These areas are considered to be densely populated and equipped with various municipal facilities. Most bike trips in such areas are usually work-based or mandatory and thus naturally less affected by weather. A moving average method is implemented to remove temporal fluctuation due to hour of day and day of week effects before analysing the impcat of weather on the Bikesharing usage.
